Integration Notes

Pipeline Intelligence pulls every Gmail message from the past 120 days, groups by sender domain, and asks Gemini Flash to synthesize account status and recommended actions. Results are posted as a rich embed to #arc-reactor Discord channel and archived to the IXON vault for historical tracking. Auto-creates "This Week" action items in Google Tasks for hot deals.
