Getting Started (2 min)

Phil will text you an invite link. Here's all you do:

1
Open the invite link Phil texts you. It looks like the URL above but with ?sync=... at the end.
2
A prompt asks "Enter your name" — type your first name (Abby, Bryce, etc.)
3
That's it. You're synced with the team. Bookmark the page.
DO NOT tap "Create New Team Sync" in Settings. Phil already created it. Just open the invite link he sends and enter your name.

When You Visit a Booth
1. Find the company (use Search or filter by Hall)
2. Tap the card to expand it
3. Set status: Visit (plan to), Visited (done), Maybe, or Skip
4. Set interest: Hot, Warm, or Cold
5. Add contact name, email/phone, notes, and next step
6. Changes auto-save and auto-sync to the team
Key Things to Know
🔒
Your data is safe. Closing Chrome does NOT lose anything. Everything saves to your device automatically.
📷
Works offline. If you lose WiFi on the show floor, keep working. Hit sync when you get signal back.
👥
Team sync. The refresh button pushes your changes up and pulls everyone else's down. Auto-syncs every few seconds too.
➕
Add new companies. Found someone not in the list? Tap the + button to add them on the fly.
📊
Export data. Tap export to download a CSV of all leads, conversations, or Apollo-ready contacts.
